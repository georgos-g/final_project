FROM BACK-TO-BACK TO INTRANET

Our current production for ŠKODA Auto Germany. We created a projection mapping show on 3 levels on behalf of the agency LMC for the ŠKODA OCTAVIA product training.

An extensive Dancing Bear in-house production: from concept to media in-house production, choreography and direction. In addition to facade and car mapping, we brought in an oversized "magic folding cube", another projection element that perfectly matches the concept. Mobile, foldable and integrated into the performance.

In the middle of the implementation we had to cancel the live shows due to the Corona Pandemic - but thanks to a resourceful customer and a great film production team the show is now running 24/7: online, on the digital training platform of ŠKODA.

There is one thing no virus in this world will change: people are driven by emotions! To evoke emotions with content is our job - and will remain so.
