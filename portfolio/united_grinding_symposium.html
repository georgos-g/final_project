Live the Elements - Unite the World

Two uniquely individual show concepts were executed by one team.

This team included MC, show director, stage managers, LED programmer, dancers, singers, musicians, circus artists, actors, and cultural artists from different countries.

Dancing Bear Productions was responsible for every aspect of the show production. Concept, show design, choreography, casting, music and video production, light plan, LED costume programming, stage management, show direction, MC and actor script.

The versatility of the artists was a key element in the effectivity of this production, as one cast could perform two different shows on two days.
