UEFA Champions League Final Ceremony

2012 Munich, Germany
  • Location:
    Alianz Arena Munich Germany
  • Performance days:
    1
  • Rehearsal days:
    4
  • Performer:
    205
  • On site team:
    16
  • Audience:
    ca. 66.000

In a league of their own!

As opening show for the finale and seen live by millions, 205 performers took to the field including star violinist David Garret and famous opera tenor Jonas Kaufmann. The performance covered the field in color and action with a surprise ending reveal of a gigantic trophy in likeness of the original.

Dancing Bear Productions played a special role in the development of the oversized stage elements in addition to the casting of the 205 performers and the choreography of the ceremony.
