Opel IAA Car Trade Fair Show

Frankfurt a. M., Germany
  • Location:
    Messe Frankfurt a. M., Germany
  • Performance days:
    13
  • Rehearsal days:
    5
  • Performer:
    13
  • On site team:
    4
  • Audience:
    ca. ca. 50 000

You can't believe your eyes!

Our performance was a combination of 2 shows on 2 stages rotating throughout the day.
The result: nonstop action for the entire 13 days of the fair.
The "twin show" (for the effect of a very fast quick change) was performed by 5 sets of identical twins 10 times per day.

The Segway show highlighted the prototype auto with Segways appearing from rear of car and was highlighted by a visit from Chancellor Angela Merkel and was performed 12 times per day. We are proud to say that we never had a late show start!
